About Me

P-VALUE CONSULTING LLC is owned and operated by Matteo Sesia, PhD, a statistician and data scientist with extensive experience in both academic research and applied consulting.

With a strong foundation in statistics and machine learning, I provide tailored solutions and expert advice for complex data science challenges. My background includes a PhD in Statistics from Stanford University, where my dissertation earned the Jerome H. Friedman Applied Statistics Dissertation Award.

In addition to my consulting work, I hold a tenure-track position as an Assistant Professor of Data Sciences and Operations at the Marshall School of Business, University of Southern California, with a courtesy appointment in Computer Science at the Viterbi School of Engineering.

Through P-VALUE CONSULTING LLC, I have assisted clients with a range of data science projects, from large-scale data mining and analysis to developing innovative uncertainty estimation methods for machine learning problems.

For more information about my academic work, please visit my personal academic webpage or my Google Scholar page.
